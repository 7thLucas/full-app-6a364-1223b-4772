Verified Operation

Weight Transaction Recorded

A vehicle is weighed in or out, the weight form is submitted and saved with timestamp and operator ID. One completed entry = one verified operation. Clicks, logins, and edits do not count.

WVO Formula

Assumption — adjust this X vehicles / day
× Assumption — adjust this operating days / week
× 2 weigh events / vehicle (in + out)
= WVO / week (fill in your numbers above)

Example: 20 vehicles/day × 5 days × 2 events = 200 verified operations/week

Value per Operation

Each transaction saved from paper to digital eliminates:

  • → Manual transcription time (~5 min/transaction saved)
  • → Risk of lost or disputed weight records
  • → Supervisor reconciliation overhead

Example derivation:
200 ops/wk × 5 min saved × staff cost
= ~16.7 staff-hours recovered / week
